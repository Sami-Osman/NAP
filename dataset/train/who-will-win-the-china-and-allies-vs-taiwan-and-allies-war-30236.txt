Discussion Name: Who will win the China (and allies) vs Taiwan (and allies) war?

1. Who will win the China \(and allies\) vs Taiwan \(and allies\) war?
1.1. Taiwan \(and allies\)
1.1.1. Con: Taiwan is small in size and can be surrounded
1.1.1.1. Pro: China is [267 times](https://www.mylifeelsewhere.com/country-size-comparison/china/taiwan) larger than Taiwan.
1.1.1.1.1. Pro: Taiwan is 35,980 sq km, while China is approximately 9,596,960 sq km.
1.1.2. Pro: Taiwan's terrain will make it [difficult](https://www.cnn.com/2019/06/23/asia/taiwan-china-invasion-intl-hnk/index.html) for China to infiltrate.
1.1.2.1. Pro: Parts of the island's east coast is considered inhabitable.
1.1.2.2. Pro: [Two-thirds](https://www.worldatlas.com/webimage/countrys/asia/taiwan/twland.htm) of Taiwan's terrain is rugged mountain landscape.
1.1.3. Pro: Taiwan has better military technology
1.1.3.1. Pro: Taiwan is [currently modernizing](https://www.defenseindustrydaily.com/taiwans-unstalled-force-modernization-04250/) its military with a P-3 maritime patrol aircraft, UH-60M helicopters, Patriot missile upgrades, and more.
1.1.3.2. Con: The sheer weight in [numbers of China's military](https://nationalinterest.org/blog/buzz/taiwans-military-really-ready-take-china-40682) will offset the small lead in technological advancement. At some point, Taiwan will be taken over by the size.
1.1.4. Con: Taiwan has a smaller military
1.1.4.1. Pro: Taiwan is ranked [49th](https://www.globalfirepower.com/available-military-manpower.asp) in military manpower, while China is first.
1.1.4.2. Con: Smaller militia have successfully [fought](https://www.ranker.com/list/small-countries-fought-off-enormous-armies/daveesons) larger ones. Taiwan thus has a possibility of winning.
1.1.5. Pro: Taiwan is backed by the Free World in general and America in specific
1.1.5.1. Pro: Taiwan and the US has [strengthened](https://thediplomat.com/2019/03/taiwan-us-plan-new-more-direct-talks-this-fall/) their ties over the years, while China and the US's alliances have weakened.
1.1.5.1.1. Con: The US [needs](https://www.industryweek.com/economy/us-needs-china-more-china-needs-us) China's alliance more than it needs Taiwan. It is therefore likely the US will try to preserve good standing with China.
1.1.5.2. Pro: Taiwan is set to receive [$2 billion](https://www.nytimes.com/2019/07/09/world/asia/taiwan-arms-sales.html) in US arms.
1.2. China \(and allies\)
1.2.1. Con: [China is on a collision course with the West](https://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2019/aug/05/hong-kong-protests-china-collision-west)
1.2.2. Pro: China has a bigger military
1.2.2.1. Pro: China ranks number 3 in air power, while [Taiwain](https://www.globalfirepower.com/country-military-strength-detail.asp?country_id=taiwan#airpower) ranks 13th in the world.
1.2.2.2. Pro: China has the [second biggest](https://www.globalfirepower.com/country-military-strength-detail.asp?country_id=taiwan#airpower) naval force, while Taiwan is 26th.
1.2.2.3. Con: -> See 1.1.4.2.
1.2.3. Pro: China has more money to spend \(bigger economy\)
1.2.3.1. Pro: China's GDP is worth [$13 trillion](https://tradingeconomics.com/china/gdp), whereas Taiwan's is only [$589 billion](https://tradingeconomics.com/taiwan/gdp).
1.2.3.2. Pro: China's military spending is set to increase by [7.5](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2019-03-05/china-s-military-spending-slows-as-economy-cools)%.
1.2.3.2.1. Con: Taiwan's budget is set to increase by [8.3](https://foreignpolicy.com/2019/08/15/taiwan-boosts-military-spending-as-china-looms/)% this year.
1.2.3.2.1.1. Con: Even with this increase, Taiwan's spending budget is still much less than China's.
1.2.4. Con: China's military is untested
1.2.5. Con: China publishes false reports on its military and economic capabilities
